Hi-Fi Audio and Me

The first pair of headphones that I bought for myself the Sony MDR-XB700 which lasted me from middle school up through my sophmore year at college. At that point, I made a huge jump to Sennheiser HD 600's with a HiFiMAN EF2C tube amp. I made it thought the better part of a year before the itch to upgrade got to me and I jumped on a pair Audeze EL-8's which are now my current daily driver. Somewhere in that mix I also picked up the Blue Mo-Fi closed-backs to everyday carry at school in order to block out noise and distractions.
Beyond that, Hi-Fi audio has also expanded into my home theater, I strive to be able to create the best cinematic experience possible. Right now, I have a modest setup with Polk RTiA7 towers, RTiA3 bookshelfs and a CSiA4 center.
